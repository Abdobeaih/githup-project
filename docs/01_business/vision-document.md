# Vision Document

## Project Name

Mustakleen Platform

---

# 1. Introduction

Mustakleen is a freelance services and discount management platform designed to connect users, companies, and administrators through a centralized digital ecosystem.

The platform provides:

* freelance and service management
* discount discovery and redemption
* installment-based service enrollment
* company analytics
* user loyalty tracking
* multilingual support

The project was originally developed as a graduation project and later transformed into a fully reverse-engineered enterprise-style software system with structured documentation and QA readiness preparation.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many users struggle to:

* discover trusted discounts and offers
* manage subscriptions and installments
* track service usage
* access centralized service providers

Companies also face difficulties in:

* publishing offers efficiently
* tracking customer engagement
* analyzing discount usage

The platform aims to solve these issues through a centralized digital marketplace.

---

# 3. Vision Statement

To build a scalable and user-friendly platform that simplifies service discovery, discount management, and customer engagement while providing companies with operational visibility and analytics.

---

# 4. Business Goals

## Primary Goals

* Provide a centralized discount and services marketplace
* Enable secure user and company onboarding
* Support multilingual experiences
* Improve customer engagement through loyalty systems
* Simplify installment-based enrollments

## Secondary Goals

* Provide analytics dashboards
* Improve accessibility to partner services
* Support future scalability and backend migration

---

# 5. Target Audience

## End Users

Individuals searching for discounts and service offers.

## Companies

Businesses that want to publish and manage offers.

## Administrators

Platform operators responsible for moderation and approvals.

---

# 6. Core Features

* User authentication and authorization
* Role-based dashboards
* Discount marketplace
* Discount redemption system
* Installment management
* Loyalty points system
* Admin approval workflows
* Localization support
* Analytics dashboards

---

# 7. Success Criteria

The platform is considered successful if:

* users can redeem discounts successfully
* companies can manage offers efficiently
* administrators can moderate content securely
* the system remains responsive and maintainable
* future QA and automation activities can be executed efficiently
